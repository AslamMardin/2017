<p><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;"><span style="line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;"><b><a href="http://jelajahinfo.com/">JELAJAHINFO.COM</a> - </b>Pada kesempatan ini akan dibagikan megenai <b><u>Cara Paling Ampuh Mempercepat Koneksi Internet Laptop/PC</u></b>. Saat ini pengguna internet berkembang pesat, baik yang menggunakan internet untuk selalu update pada media sosial, membaca berita online, game online, dan kegiatan internet lainnya. &nbsp;</span></span></span><br />
<span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;"><span style="line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;"><br /></span></span></span>
<span style="color: #444444;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;">Namun, untuk bisa nyaman melakukan kegiatan berselancar di dunia internet maka dibutuhkan koneksi yang cepat. Seperti yang kita ketahui bersama bahwa, koneksi internet Indonesia merupakan salah satu koneksi internet yang paling lambat. Oleh karena itu, untuk memperoleh koneksi yang lebih baik maka dibutuhkan sedikit trik.&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="background-color: white; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;">&nbsp;T</span><span style="background-color: white; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;">rik ini tidak selalu berhasil karena tergantung pada situasi dan lokasi anda, jika internetnya memang lagi down di server pusat, maka segala macam trik yang di lakukan akan sama aja hasilnya, berikut triknya:</span></span><br />
<span style="color: #444444;"><br /></span>
<h2 style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; padding: 0px; text-align: center; vertical-align: baseline;">
<span style="color: #444444; font-size: small;">Cara Paling Ampuh Mempercepat Koneksi Internet Laptop/PC</span></h2>
<span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;"><span style="line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;">Silahkan ikuti langkah-langkahnya berikut.</span></span></span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #444444;"><br /></span></span>
<span style="background-color: white; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #444444;">1. Untuk pengguna Laptop/PC yang menggunakan sistem Operasi Windows, maka anda hanya bisa menggunakan maksimal koneksi internet 80%, sisanya 20% untuk menjalankan fungsi-fungsi lain. Oleh karenanya, untuk bisa menggunakannya 100% maka anda harus melakukan proses edit pada file konfigurasi di gpedit.msc. Untuk langkah-langkahnya silahkan ikuti dibawah ini.</span></span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #444444;"><ul>
<li><span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Tekan tombol Windows + R, maka akan muncul tab Run, kemudian ketik </span><span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><b>gpedit.msc</b></span></li>
</ul>

</ul>

<li><span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Klik pada bagian Enable, kemudian ganti bandwidth limit menjadi 0.</span></li>

</span><span style="background-color: white; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: inherit;">2. <b style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><a href="http://www.jelajahinfo.com/2015/05/cara-paling-ampuh-mempercepat-koneksi-internet.html" target="_blank">cara mempercepat koneksi internet</a></b>&nbsp;untuk pengguna modem GSM, ini beberapa tips nya.</span></span></span><ul style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;">
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Gunakanlah modem yang memiliki kecepatan tinggi, lebih baik menggunakan modem berkecepatan diatas 7.2mbps, karena kecepatan modem sangat mempengaruhi kecepatan internet.</span></span></li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Kalo ada, gunakanlah antena atau alat lain nya untuk memperkuat sinyal modem anda, dan lebih baik menggunakan kipas agar modem tidak cepat panas.&nbsp;</span></span></li>
</ul>
<span style="color: #444444;"><span style="background-color: white; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;">3. Mensetting port pada windows, carannya yaitu</span></span><ol style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;"></ol>
<span style="background-color: white; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #444444;"><ul>
<li><span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Klik Start &gt; Klik kanan pada My computer, &gt; Pilih Properties &gt; lalu pilih Device Manager.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Setelah itu cari Port (COM &amp; LPT), double klik pada salah satu port.</span></li>
</ul>

</span><ol style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;"></ol>
<span style="color: #444444;"><span style="background-color: white; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><ul>
<li><span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Pilih tab port setting, ganti bit per second menjadi 128000, dan flow control menjadi Hardware, lakukan hal ini pada port lainnya nya juga.</span></li>
</ul>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rbT3vNBgn_w/VUbjp_2sHPI/AAAAAAAAA6M/Hit89lHB9x8/s1600/mempercepat%2Bkoneksi%2Binternet4.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rbT3vNBgn_w/VUbjp_2sHPI/AAAAAAAAA6M/Hit89lHB9x8/s1600/mempercepat%2Bkoneksi%2Binternet4.JPG" height="320" width="289" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
</span><span style="background-color: white; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="border-image-outset: initial; border-image-repeat: initial; border-image-slice: initial; border-image-source: initial; border-image-width: initial; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="border-image-outset: initial; border-image-repeat: initial; border-image-slice: initial; border-image-source: initial; border-image-width: initial; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="border-image-outset: initial; border-image-repeat: initial; border-image-slice: initial; border-image-source: initial; border-image-width: initial; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="font-style: inherit;">4.</span><span style="font-style: inherit; font-weight: bold;"> </span>M</span></span></span><span style="font-style: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">ensetting Prioritas Provider, untuk merubahnya masuklah ke regedit dengan cara:</span></span></span></span><ul style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;">
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Tekan Windows+R, lalu ketikkan regedit dan enter.</span></span></li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Pada tab sebelah kiri, pilih HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE &gt;&gt; SYSTEM &gt;&gt; CurrentControlSet &gt;&gt; Services &gt;&gt; Tcpip &gt;&gt; ServiceProvider.</span></span><div class="separator" style="border: 0px; clear: both; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: center; vertical-align: baseline;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-rm8ojXOYQoU/VAP2MD9tblI/AAAAAAAABIY/N4KQlkPITbE/s1600/c6.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="-webkit-transition: all 0.5s; background-color: white; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px 1em; outline: none; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none; transition: all 0.5s; vertical-align: baseline;" title="mempercepat koneksi internet"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit; font-size: small;"></span></a></div>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7W6jFOww7xM/VUbj92ug2ZI/AAAAAAAAA6c/Ba89M95WRTE/s1600/mempercepat%2Bkoneksi%2Binternet5.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><span style="color: #444444;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7W6jFOww7xM/VUbj92ug2ZI/AAAAAAAAA6c/Ba89M95WRTE/s1600/mempercepat%2Bkoneksi%2Binternet5.JPG" height="212" width="320" /></span></a></div>
<ul style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;">
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Lalu disebelah kanan, double klik pada DnsPriority, ganti value data menjadi 1, begitu juga HostsPriority, LocalPriority, NetbtPriority, ganti value nya menjadi 1 semua.</span></span></li>
</ul>
<span style="background-color: white; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">5. Menggunakan Tweak cpip Parameter, untuk melakukan tweak, kita perlu memasukkan registry baru kedalam sistem regedit, cara nya seperti ini:</span></span><ul style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;">
<li style="border-image-outset: initial; border-image-repeat: initial; border-image-slice: initial; border-image-source: initial; border-image-width: initial; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Buka notepad, lalu copy kode berikut ini dan pastekan dalam notepad.</span></span></li>
</ul>
<blockquote class="tr_bq">
<span style="color: #444444;">[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINESYSTEMCurrentControlSetServicesT cpipParameters]<br />&#8220;SackOpts&#8221; = dword: 00000001<br />&#8220;TcpWindowSize&#8221; = dword: 0005ae4c<br />&#8220;Tcp1323Opts&#8221; = dword: 00000003<br />&#8220;DefaultTTL&#8221; = dword: 00000040<br />&#8220;EnablePMTUBHDetect&#8221; = dword: 00000000<br />&#8220;EnablePMTUDiscovery&#8221; = dword: 00000001<br />&#8220;GlobalMaxTcpWindowSize&#8221; = dword: 0005ae4c</span></blockquote>
<ul style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;">
<li style="border-image-outset: initial; border-image-repeat: initial; border-image-slice: initial; border-image-source: initial; border-image-width: initial; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">&nbsp;Lalu simpan dengan nama Tweak.reg . dan jalankan file tersebut.</span></span></li>
</ul>
<span style="background-color: white; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">6. Menggunakan Dns lain,&nbsp;<u style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><b style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">cara mempercepat koneksi internet</b></u>&nbsp;dengan cara ini hanya untuk yang Dns nya sering error ataupun slow respon, jadi sobat bisa mengganti nya dengan Dns Google ataupun Open Dns.</span></span><ul style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; list-style-position: outside; margin: 0px 0px 1.2em 3em; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; vertical-align: baseline;">
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Menggunakan Dns Google, Buka Control panel dan pilih network.</span></span></li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Lalu klik pada nama koneksi sobat.</span></span><div class="separator" style="border: 0px; clear: both; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: center; vertical-align: baseline;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-87VnPNU_nrM/VAQGL6sMoCI/AAAAAAAABIo/yusYVPl3NGQ/s1600/n1.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="-webkit-transition: all 0.5s; background-color: white; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px 1em; outline: none; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none; transition: all 0.5s; vertical-align: baseline;" title="mempercepat koneksi internet mudah"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit; font-size: small;"><img alt="mempercepat koneksi internet mudah" border="0" height="114" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-87VnPNU_nrM/VAQGL6sMoCI/AAAAAAAABIo/yusYVPl3NGQ/s1600/n1.JPG" style="border: 0px none; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; outline: none; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;" title="mempercepat koneksi internet mudah" width="320" /></span></a></div>
</li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Klik Properties &gt; Internet Protocol Version 4 &gt; Pilih Use following DNS &gt; isikan DNS Google yaitu 8.8.8.8 dan 8.8.4.4</span></span><div class="separator" style="border: 0px; clear: both; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: center; vertical-align: baseline;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-5UtKGg025w4/VAQGMnX6DXI/AAAAAAAABIs/s1xtrB7nlYM/s1600/n2.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="-webkit-transition: all 0.5s; background-color: white; border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px 1em; outline: none; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none; transition: all 0.5s; vertical-align: baseline;" title="cara mempercepat koneksi internet speedy"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit; font-size: small;"><img alt="cara mempercepat koneksi internet speedy" border="0" height="173" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-5UtKGg025w4/VAQGMnX6DXI/AAAAAAAABIs/s1xtrB7nlYM/s1600/n2.JPG" style="border: 0px none; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; outline: none; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;" title="cara mempercepat koneksi internet speedy" width="320" /></span></a></div>
</li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Klik ok sampai selesai.</span></span></li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Dns Google juga bisa digunakan untuk menembus website yang diblokir oleh Dns ISP anda.</span></span></li>
<li style="border: 0px; font-stretch: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">Anda juga bisa menggunakan OpenDns, yaitu 208.67.222.222 dan 208.67.220.220</span></span></li>
</ul>
<span style="color: #444444;"><span style="background-color: white;"><span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;">Untuk memunculkan efeknya, maka anda harus merestart ulang Laptop/PC anda, itulah&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="line-height: 19.6000003814697px;"><i style="font-weight: bold;">Cara Paling Ampuh Mempercepat Koneksi Internet Laptop/PC</i>. Perlu diingatkan kembali bahwa trik diatas tidak kesemuanya berhasil,&nbsp;</span><span style="background-color: white; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify;">mungkin memang internet dari provider anda sangat lambat.
